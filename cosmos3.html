Cosmos



You are a blue ball in a galaxy! Use keyboard keys to move.

Avoid white bubbles flying in. They can invade from anywhere!

------- TWO WAYS TO PLAY -------

RUNAWAY - Focus on just evading attackers by running away

SNIPER - avaoid attackers and shoot at the invaders by clicking!

M = Mute Music

R = Restart Game

P = Pause Game

1 = Runaway

2 = Sniper


Press ENTER now to start!
